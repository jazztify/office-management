The Mandatory Core (The Concrete Foundation)
Module 0: Identity & Multi-Tenancy: Super Admin billing, Workspace generation, Role-Based Access Control (RBAC), and the JSON module toggles. (You cannot turn this off, or the app ceases to exist). 👻🏢

💳 Commerce & Finance (The Money Engine)
Module 1: Universal POS & Shift Management: The tablet cash register. Handles transactions, cash drawer floats, end-of-day Z-Out reports, and auto-calculates tier discounts or loyalty rewards. 🛒💵

Module 2: Digital Wallet & Ledger: Strictly for cashless ecosystems (like your Pickleball VIPs). Tracks member top-ups, deducts balances, and prevents negative spending. 💳🏦

Module 3: Returns, Refunds & RMA: The retail lifesaver. Processes returned items, issues store credit, and fixes the financial ledgers when a customer changes their mind. 🔄📉

📦 Operations & Supply Chain
Module 4: HR, Payroll & Commissions: Employee profiles, attendance logs, and that insane auto-calculating Coach Commission Matrix (70/30 splits, etc.). 👔⚖️

Module 5: Advanced Inventory & Multi-Warehouse: The retail beast. Tracks SKUs, size/color variants, barcode generation, low-stock alerts, and branch-to-branch stock transfers. 📦🚚

🏸 Gym & Service Specific (The Pickleball Fortress)
Module 6: Club Memberships & Subscriptions: Tracks your Silver, Gold, and Platinum tiers, handles recurring billing, and assigns physical RFID/NFC cards to member profiles. 🏅🎟️

Module 7: Booking & Scheduling: The calendar engine. Prevents two $50,000 Platinum members from booking the same court and starting a riot. Handles private coach time slots. 📅🏓

Module 8: Access Control & Hardware (IoT): The physical bouncer. Listens to smart locks and turnstiles, checks the database for active subscriptions, and enforces anti-passback rules. 🚪📡

🤝 Customer Engagement & Retention
Module 9: CRM & Marketing Automation: The silent salesperson. Automatically blasts SMS or emails when a subscription is expiring in 3 days or a wallet balance drops below $10. 📧🎯

Module 10: Loyalty & Rewards Program: The retail hook. "Spend $100, get 10 points." Tracks customer lifetime value and generates discount codes. 🎁⭐

Module 11: The End-User Portal: A separate, lightweight mobile web app where members/customers can actually log in to check their own wallet balance or book a court without bothering the front desk. 📱🌐

Module 12: Multi-Social Marketing Automation: The distribution engine. Create a single announcement or promotion and instantly broadcast it to TikTok, Facebook, YouTube, Instagram, and more. 🚀📢
